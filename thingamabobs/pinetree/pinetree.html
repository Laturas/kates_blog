Pinetree MP3 Player

By Isokate

This is the official download/info page for Pinetree - a lightweight desktop MP3 player application.

Pinetree was built so that I could track statistics on what music I listen to and such while also escaping the grasp of services like Spotify and Youtube Music. There are some admittedly unusual design decisions, as I originally only intended for this project to be used by myself, but it has now been released publicly due to demand.

Pinetree tracks how long you listen to each song and stores it as a csv. For those worried, Pinetree runs entirely locally and does not share any data with anyone.
